Shiva Temple

The Shiva temple also known as "Rani Temple" or "Maharani Temple", is situated on a small hillock in the centre of Gulmarg. The temple became famous when bollywood number "Jai Jai Shiva Shanker" was shot here in early 1970s featuring Rajesh Khanna - Mumtaz.

Gulmarg Gandola

Gondola ride in Gulmarg is perhaps the most awesome when it comes to views. The ride is split in two stages and both require diff tickets. Tickets can be sourced online. Both stages are a must do. The Gondola ride is subject to weather.

Gulmarg Biosphere Reserve

Gulmarg Biosphere Reserve is home to many common and rare species of fauna, flora and avifauna. Located at an altitude varying between 2400 and 4,300 metres above sea level, this reserve is among those regions where biodiversity is in its richest form. Nature has blessed this sanctuary with expansive patches of conifers and green herbs that give it a scenic appearance.
